CA Foundation May 2026 Exam Postponed

Published on April 2026

The Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI) has officially announced that the CA Foundation examination has been postponed.

New Exam Schedule

The examination is delayed and the date of examination will be announced shortly on the official site of ICAI.

Important Notice

All previously issued admit cards stand cancelled. Fresh admit cards will be issued for the revised schedule.

What Students Should Do

  • Keep checking the official ICAI website
  • Continue preparation
  • Download new admit cards once released
